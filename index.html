What is StAPP?

Student Assistant App (StAPP) facilitates undergraduates to keep track of everything related to their degree program. From the time of waking up in the morning, until the dawn of the next day - a student can record all his academic activities from attending a lecture to referring the subject matter he was taught. Hence, the student can get an idea about where he stands at any given time.

Features



Attendance

Daily attendance for lectures can be recorded including absented sessions, cancellations, holidays, vacations and unpredicted close-downs. Hence, the student can know the percentage of attendance at any given time.


Time Table

Once the course units relevant for the semester is fed to StAPP at the beginning along with the lecture schedule, StAPP creates the time-table for the user. Hence, facilitating the user to record his daily activities.


Course Content

Students can easily record daily work (i.e - learned lessons, referred lessons, completed tutorials & tried past papers) in the user-friendly framework provided by StAPP. Hence, students need not remember their daily activities as StAPP provides a clear overview of it at any given time.


GPA

StAPP at the end of each semester can be fed with grades obtained for each course unit - hence, providing the student with a source to know his updated GPA by the end of each semester and also at any given time.
